FLYBY

Making group travel plans can be stressful and time consuming, and poor communication within the group can affect the overall travel experience.

Research Design Final

Team

Solo Project

Design Role

UX Designer, UX Researcher, Visual Design, Brand & Identity.

Tools

Figma, Invision.

Overview

To create an app that allows you to share your travel plans, itinerary, and documents with your traveling friends and family. While also providing access to all travel-related information and tips. Using a simple user journey to help users achieve their objectives.

Initial Research
Interviews & Surveys Conducted. Proto Persona. Results created into affinity diagram, then empathy map, focused on user pain points and gains. Created a user persona.
Definition & Synthesis

User insight statement and problem statement.

Our travel planning app was designed to help users plan collaborative group holidays after the pandemic quickly and effectively. We have observed that our product isn’t allowing users access to all guidelines regarding post pandemic regulations, which is causing the users to use other platforms to access information which is decreasing the number of users. How might we improve our travel planning app so that our customers are successful based on increased usage and traffic on the post pandemic regulations page?

Design Phase
Ideation process using I like, I wish, what if method. Feature prioritisation matrix. Value proposition. User scenario, story boarding and user journey map to visualise the app process. Received feedback and made improvements.
Prototyping

User flow diagram for onboarding process. Low Fi wireframes and clickable prototype for initial user tests.

User Testing
Initial user tests held to gain feedback. Received feedback and made changes to the high Fi wireframes and carried out more user tests to ensure the app works smoothly. Received positive feedback for the high Fi prototype.

Final Thoughts

During this project, I had lots of positive and negative experiences that helped me shape and develop my app to its potential. The aim of my study was to create an app that will help users plan group trips without the stress and confusion. I do believe that I achieved this by implementing my main feature into the app early on, this is the Group Travel feature that uses a share code to share travel plans with other users on the app.
